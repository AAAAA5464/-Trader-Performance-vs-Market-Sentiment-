# Trader Performance vs Market Sentiment

This project analyzes the relationship between Bitcoin market sentiment (Fear/Greed Index) and trader performance on Hyperliquid. The goal is to identify patterns between market psychology and trading outcomes.

## Objectives
- Analyze how market sentiment affects trading behavior
- Compare trader performance during Fear vs Greed periods
- Identify patterns that could improve trading strategies

## Tools & Technologies
- Python
- Pandas
- NumPy
- Matplotlib / Seaborn
- Google Colab

## Datasets
1. Bitcoin Market Sentiment (Fear & Greed Index)
2. Hyperliquid Trader Performance Dataset

## Key Insights
- Traders tend to take higher risks during Greed periods
- Fear periods often show reduced trading activity
- Market sentiment has a measurable impact on trading performance
